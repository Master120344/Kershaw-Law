Our Guiding Mission

Our mission at The Kershaw Law Firm P.C. is to provide direct legal guidance and strategic solutions for U.S. employers building their workforce through the H-2A and H-2B visa programs. We are driven by a commitment to integrity, thoroughness, and achieving outcomes that support your business operations.

We understand that navigating U.S. immigration law can be challenging. Our firm was founded to clarify this process, offering clear, actionable advice and serving as a steadfast advocate for your interests from initial consultation to final adjudication and beyond.

Dedicated H-2A & H-2B Focus

The landscape of temporary work visas is always evolving. The Kershaw Law Firm P.C. maintains a singular focus on the H-2A (agricultural) and H-2B (non-agricultural) visa categories. This specialization allows us to maintain a comprehensive understanding of:

  • Regulatory requirements from the Department of Labor (DOL), USCIS, and other governing bodies.
  • Evolving policy interpretations and adjudication trends that affect petition success.
  • The critical timelines and procedures essential for timely workforce deployment.
  • Proactive compliance strategies to mitigate risks and ensure long-term program viability for your business.

We partner with you to understand your unique business needs and develop visa strategies tailored to your specific industry, whether it's agriculture, landscaping, hospitality, construction, or another sector reliant on a seasonal workforce.
